What I Learned

In this semester of intro to computing, I gained valuable knowledge about the fundamentals of technology and computing. I learned about the technology timeline, which outlined key developments from early computing machines to modern technologies, helping me understand the rapid evolution of the field. The course also covered the information processing cycle, explaining how computers process data through input, processing, storage, and output. We explored the components of computer systems, learning about both hardware (like CPUs and storage devices) and software (such as operating systems and applications). The course also introduced cybercrime, including types like hacking, phishing, and identity theft, as well as the profiles of cybercriminals. This made me realize the importance of computer security, such as using firewalls, encryption, and strong passwords to protect data. Finally, I learned how to make wireframes, its significance to website designing and planning. I also learned the basics of HTML and CSS, which are used to build and style websites. This course provided a well-rounded foundation, enhancing my understanding of how technology impacts daily life and the need for security in the digital world.

Reflection

Partaking Intro to Computing has been a great learning experience for me. I was amazed to discover how early technologies, like the abacus, evolved into supercomputers and eventually into the small, powerful devices we use today. It really highlighted how much technology has advanced over time. I also learned how computers process data through the information processing cycle, which involves input, processing, storage, and output. Understanding how the different parts of computer systems like hardware and software work together was eye opening. The course also introduced me to cybercrime, including threats like hacking and identity theft, and the importance of computer security to protect our information. Lastly, learning the basics of HTML and CSS gave me a hands-on look at how websites are created and designed. Overall, this course helped me understand how technology works, how far it has come, and why securing our digital lives is so important. It sparked my interest in computing, and I’m excited to keep learning.
